Executions

The following configuration shows how to look up a username/password from your settings.xml. You can then use these credentials in your pom e.g. in a Jetty JNDI definition, or as a system property in a unit test.

<project>
  [...]
  <build>
    [...]
    <plugins>
      <plugin>
        <groupId>org.codehaus.mojo</groupId>
        <artifactId>credentials-maven-plugin</artifactId>
        <version>1.0-SNAPSHOT</version>

                                <configuration>
                                        <usernameProperty>username</usernameProperty>
                                        <passwordProperty>password</passwordProperty>
                                        <settingsKey>my.server</settingsKey>
                                        <useSystemProperties>true</useSystemProperties>
                                </configuration>
                                
      </plugin>
      [...]
    </plugins>
    [...]
  </build>
  [...]
</project>
